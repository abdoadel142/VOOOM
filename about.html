Company background:

Vooom was founded in 2020, with a purpose of creating smart solutions to simplify every day of people’s lives. Vooom is a delivery solution service provider which simplifies errands for businesses and individuals and helps save couriering hassle and expenses in order to simplify business operations & every day in their lives. We also provide you with smart software solutions for businesses to offer a simpler business operation experience. .


Definition of the brand name:

Vooom is the sound of the bike that resembles our speedy & easy delivery solution that is derived from our couriers’ bikes which helps us deliver your orders as easy, time & cost saving as possible.
